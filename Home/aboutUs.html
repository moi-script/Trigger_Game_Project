Trigger Game is a project by first-year engineering students for Engineering Week. It's designed to be a fun and educational way for students to learn and review important engineering concepts. Developed by John Moises A. Nugal, Zymund Orillo, and Rimer Lupian, this game combines their skills and efforts to create an enjoyable learning tool for their peers.
